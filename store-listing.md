**Name:** Xpress Printing Toolkit

**Summary (132 chars max):**
Print shop toolkit: instant quotes in TZS, print-quality checker for any image, WhatsApp quote composer. English & Swahili.

**Description:**

The everyday toolkit for print shops — built by XPRESS PRINTING TANZANIA (Moshi, Kilimanjaro).

⚡ INSTANT QUOTES
Price banners, large-format prints and stickers per square metre; t-shirts with automatic bulk discounts; business cards, mugs, teardrop banners and logo design. Build a multi-line quotation in seconds. Every price is editable, so any print shop can use it with its own price list and currency.

🔍 PRINT-QUALITY CHECKER
Clients send low-resolution images all the time. Drop any image into the checker — or right-click an image anywhere on the web and choose "Check print quality" — and instantly see its pixel size and the maximum size it can be printed at 300 DPI (cards & flyers), 150 DPI (posters) and 75 DPI (banners), with a clear verdict. No more blurry banners and awkward reprints.

💬 WHATSAPP QUOTES
One click turns your quote into a neatly formatted WhatsApp message and opens a chat from your configured business number. Copy-to-clipboard fallback included.

🌍 ENGLISH & KISWAHILI
Switch the interface language any time.

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Works fully offline. No accounts, no analytics, no uploads — images are analyzed inside your browser and settings stay on your device.
