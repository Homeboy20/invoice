# Privacy Policy — Xpress Printing Toolkit

_Last updated: 2026-07-23_

Xpress Printing Toolkit does **not** collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any
personal data or usage data.

- All settings (business name, WhatsApp number, price list, language) are stored only in
  your browser via `chrome.storage.sync`, which Chrome may sync between your own signed-in
  browsers. They never reach any server operated by us.
- Images you check are analyzed entirely inside your browser. They are never uploaded.
- The extension makes no network requests of its own. The only outbound navigation is the
  WhatsApp link (`wa.me`) opened when you explicitly click "Send via WhatsApp".
- No analytics, no tracking, no cookies, no remote code.

Permissions used:
- `storage` — save your settings locally.
- `contextMenus` — add the right-click "Check print quality" item on images.

Contact: via https://xpressprinting.co.tz
